You and another user (or a bot) will negotiate the price of an item for sale.

Instructions - Please read carefully!

  • Your role (buyer or seller) is to the right, as well as the description of an item for sale and a photo (if available).
  • Use the chat box below to negotiate with your partner given the description on the right. Please use complete, grammatical English without typos.
  • Please do not leave the chat unattended. If you are inactive for more than 3 minutes your connection will time out.
  • Feel free to negotiate terms that are not financial! For e.g., offering to pick up the item yourself; throwing in free items; negotiating additional benefits like a warranty or utilities.
  • Be creative, but don't contradict any facts given in the description or shown in the image!
  • During the chat, please tag your partner's utterance when it is not grammatical, out-of-context (incoherent), contradicting what was said previously, or when you find it is persuasive.
  • At the end of the negotiation, submit the agreed deal in the text box at right.
  • After you submit an offer, your partner can accept or reject it.
  • If you run into any trouble with the website, use the button on the top right to report the issue.
