You and another user online will negotiate the price of a used car.

Instructions

  • Your role (buyer or seller) is to the right.
  • Your expected price range is provided as well. We will award bonuses to those who get a better deal!
  • Use the chat box below to negotiate with your partner given the description on the right.
  • When you and your partner have agreed on a price, enter it in the text box and hit 'Submit'.
  • NOTE: You have only one chance to offer; the price must be a multitude of 100.
  • You must agree on a final price within the time limit in order to receive credit for this HIT.
  • Please do not state facts that are not explicitly given to you. It's okay to embellish certain aspects as a negotiation tactic!
  • To the seller:
    • It's okay to embellish facts, but don't make up new properties not provided to you (or contradict given properties).
    • Do: It's ready to drive across the country.
    • Don't do: (if the facts did not mention recent tire replacement) I have just replaced all tires.
  • To the buyer:
    • It's okay to make up preferences as a negotiation tactic, but don't make up properties that are not provided (or contradict given properties).
    • Do: It has a high milleage and I'm worried about the functionality of the tire and the brake.
    • Don't do: (if the facts did not mention a broken window) The window is broken.
